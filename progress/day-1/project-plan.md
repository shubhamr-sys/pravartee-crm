**Project Duration:** 24 Weeks
**Start Date:** 03 June 2026

---

# Executive Summary

The objective of this project is to design, develop, deploy, and maintain a centralized business platform for Pravartee Sales Pvt. Ltd.

The platform will provide:

* Lead Management
* Sales Pipeline Tracking
* Sales Reporting
* Government Tender Monitoring
* AI-Powered Business Automation

The solution will be implemented in multiple phases to reduce risk and ensure continuous business value delivery.

---

# Project Goals

## Primary Goals

* Centralize sales lead management
* Improve follow-up discipline
* Increase visibility into sales activities
* Improve tender discovery and tracking
* Automate recurring reporting tasks
* Enable data-driven business decisions

## Secondary Goals

* Reduce manual effort
* Improve operational efficiency
* Create a scalable technology foundation
* Enable future mobile applications

# Success Criteria

The project shall be considered successful when:

* Lead tracking is fully operational.
* Sales activities are recorded digitally.
* CEO dashboard provides real-time visibility.
* Tender monitoring operates automatically.
* AI reporting functions without manual intervention.
* Production environment is stable and documented.
